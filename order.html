9/11 Interview Page

Within this website you will find information relating to my interview with my Interviewee, Falgun Shah, with his experience during the attack of 9/11. 9/11 was one of the deadliest attcks America has ever faced and left a lot of Americans scared.

*Made with Consent of Interviewee

Falgun Shah's Experience

Falgun Shah lived about a half hour away from New York City. He described looking at the Twin Towers falling on TV as one of the scariest things. He said that people ran around with no idea of what was going to happen. They left everything behind when they heard what happened. The only thing that mattered was to make sure their families and them were safe. Uncertainty was undenyable in their minds.
